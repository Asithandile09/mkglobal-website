Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025

MK Global SA (Pty) Ltd is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

01

Information We Collect

We collect personal information voluntarily submitted through our website, including but not limited to:

  • Full Name and Surname
  • ID Number
  • Contact Details (Phone and Email)
  • Driver's Licence Information
  • PdP Status
  • Vehicle and Operator Information (where applicable)
  • Work Preference and Operating Area
02

Purpose of Collection

Your personal information is collected for the following purposes:

  • Driver recruitment and assessment
  • Owner-driver / operator onboarding
  • Compliance verification
  • Operational coordination
  • Communication regarding opportunities
03

Lawful Basis for Processing

Personal information is processed based on your voluntary consent and for legitimate business and compliance purposes in line with POPIA.

04

Data Protection and Security

MK Global SA implements reasonable technical and organisational measures to safeguard personal information against loss, misuse, unauthorised access, disclosure, or alteration.

05

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information may only be shared with relevant operational partners where necessary for recruitment or contractual compliance.

06

Retention of Information

Personal information will be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for recruitment, compliance, or operational purposes, unless otherwise required by law.

07

Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of information where applicable
  • Withdraw consent (subject to legal or operational limitations)
08

Contact Information
